Here are project ideas I have in increasing order of complexity.

# Breakdown

## 1) Decision Tree Regression vs Symbolic Regression

Generate fake data with a known underlying symbolic relationship, then compare how well a decision tree and a symbolic regression model can approximate that relationship.

## 2) Concept Drift Aside

Focus on using symbolic regression to discover physical laws, governing equations, and relationships in data. Start by comparing a decision tree's symbolic formula output to its predictions.

## 3) Random Forest Comparison

Extend the approach to random forests and compare their predictions against symbolic regression models. Consider using local symbolic regression models to approximate the behavior of a random forest in specific regions, then compare these local models to the forest's predictions.

## 4) Local Symbolic Models

Fit local symbolic regression models for different regions of a random forest's prediction space. Compare these localized symbolic models to the random forest predictions in their respective regions.

## 5) Periodic Retraining Analysis

Given a random forest model that gets retrained periodically, fit local symbolic models at each time step and track how these models change over time. This provides a more interpretable way to monitor drift by showing which regions and equations are changing.

## 6) Symbolic Formula Comparison

Compare the symbolic formulas generated over time against the random forest predictions to track concept drift.
